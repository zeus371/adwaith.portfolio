About Me

I’m Adwaith, a cybersecurity student and ethical hacker passionate about uncovering and mitigating security vulnerabilities before they become real-world threats. My interests center on bug bounty hunting, OSINT (Open Source Intelligence), penetration testing, and red teaming — exploring how systems can be breached and, more importantly, how they can be secured.

I approach cybersecurity as both an art and a science — combining analytical thinking with creative problem-solving to identify attack paths others might overlook. From reconnaissance and exploitation to reporting and remediation, I enjoy working through the full lifecycle of a security assessment.

Outside structured engagements, I actively participate in CTF competitions, contribute to open-source security projects, and continuously develop new tools to enhance my workflow. My goal is to grow into a well-rounded security professional capable of defending modern digital infrastructures through offensive insight and ethical application.
